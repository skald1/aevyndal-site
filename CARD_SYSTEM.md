# ÆVYNDAL CARD SYSTEM

## Purpose

Cards are windows.

Not containers.

Not boxes.

They allow the world to show through.

---

# Philosophy

Cards belong to the world.

They do not sit above it.

Media should fade into content.

No hard image boundaries.

---

# Structure

Media

↓

Gradient fade

↓

Content

↓

Relationships

Everything should feel continuous.

---

# Glass

Opacity:

8–18%

Radius:

24px

Border:

1px subtle white

Noise:

Very slight

---

# Hover

Brighten

1.02 scale

Border clarity increases

Image drifts slightly

Nothing bounces

---

# Media

Ambient video preferred

Poster fallback required

Darkened

Desaturated

Slow

No action

---

# Relationship Cards

Never:

More Articles

Instead:

Nearby Places

Related Creatures

People Who Live Here

Campaigns Set Here

Cards create journeys.

---

# Aspect Ratios

Hero:

21:9

Large Cards:

16:9

Relationship Cards:

4:3

Portrait Cards:

3:4

Maintain consistency.

---

# Final Rule

Cards are windows into the world.

Not UI boxes.
