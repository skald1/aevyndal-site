# ÆVYNDAL SEO STRATEGY

## Purpose

SEO exists to help people discover Ævyndal.

SEO does not determine the experience.

The experience determines the SEO.

Never sacrifice user experience for search engines.

Serve people first.

Search engines second.

---

# Principle

Cognitive journey and SEO should support each other.

Not compete.

Every page should answer a human question.

Pages rank because they solve problems.

Not because they contain keywords.

---

# Search Intent Hierarchy

People arrive asking:

## What is this?

↓

## What stories exist here?

↓

## Put me somewhere.

↓

## Who could I be?

↓

## Tell me more.

↓

## Can I use this?

↓

## Can I belong?

These questions define site structure.

---

# Article Philosophy

Articles answer questions.

Examples:

How do people bury their dead?

What do Mütvians fear?

What creatures stalk the forest?

What do witch hunters believe?

What is life like in Cultrek?

Search intent should drive titles.

Not internal terminology.

---

# Titles

Prefer:

Life in Hallowmere

The Funeral Customs of Mütvia

Why People Fear the Scarmakers

What It Means to Be Th'il

Avoid:

On The Cultural Implications Of...

Avoid academic titles.

---

/ages/fourth-age

Readable URLs.

Never IDs.
