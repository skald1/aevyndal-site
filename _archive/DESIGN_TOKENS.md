# ÆVYNDAL DESIGN TOKENS

## Purpose

This document defines the visual constants of the website.

Components change.

Content changes.

Ages change.

Tokens remain.

Tokens create consistency.

---

# Philosophy

The world changes.

The archive remains.

Tokens are the archive.

Tokens should rarely change.

---

# Glass

Background:

Very dark.

Opacity:

8-18%

Blur:

16-30px

Border:

1px

Color:

Very subtle white.

Avoid obvious glass.

Glass should be felt.

Not noticed.

---

# Shadows

Use soft shadows.

Avoid hard shadows.

Avoid elevation systems.

Cards float.

They do not stack.
