# ÆVYNDAL TRANSITION SYSTEM

## Purpose

Transitions should feel like travel.

Never loading.

Never theme switching.

The world changes around the user.

---

# Principle

Atmosphere transitions continuously.

Information transitions discretely.

---

# Backgrounds

Crossfade.

Morph.

Remain alive.

Never disappear.

---

# Text

Old text fades and lowers.

New text enters later.

Text transitions independently.

---

# Cards

Cards remain.

Media changes.

Mood changes.

The world passes through them.

---

# Age Changes

Fourth Age

Fog

Moonlight

Roots

↓

Silver cools

Rain begins

Stars emerge

City lights replace branches

↓

Seventh Age

No blackout.

No flash.

No blur layer.

---

# Timing

Text

250–500ms

Cards

400–700ms

Atmosphere

1000–1500ms

Particles

Continuous

---

# Motion Rules

No loading screens

No bounce

No page flashes

No scroll-jacking

No fake physics

No spectacle

---

# Desired Feeling

The user never leaves.

They simply realize they are somewhere else.

---

# Final Rule

Transitions should create travel.

Not animation.
